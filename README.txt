Encyclopedia word game

The essence of the game : 

The moderator finds the word in Encyclopedia that is unknown to all teams , then he writes 4 deffinitions into the appropriate files(deffinition.txt).The task if each of the playersis to write their own defiinitions of this word from their imagine and disgusing text as a real small encyclopedic article .

Then all "articles" are mixed including the real deffinition and teams vote for which article is the most convincing.In the end team that scored  
the most votes wins the round , after 4 rounds the winner of the game determined.
